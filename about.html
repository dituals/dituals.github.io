colophon

I created this website as an antidote to soulless websites, apps, and services on the corporate web.

The content I am publishing here only belongs to me.

I share what I want to share with others.

There are no ads.

I do not use AI or any other dehumanizing technology.

I am not a fan of Hugo, Jekyll, or other static site generators. I have tried them, I don't have anything against them, I just want to keep it straight-forward.

This is my workflow:

  • I write my notes in Markdown. I use Obsidian to organize them.
  • To publish notes and deploy my site, I have a simple set-up, using GitHub and GitHub Pages.

You may have already noticed that this website is fairly simple. I created it from scratch and didn't use any templates, or custom CSS. My CSS file is less than 1KB.

I could say it's because I wanted to create a minimalist website. Truthfully, I have zero experience in "web development". Watch how this website will evolve in the next months and years (while still maintaining simplicity).

Feel free to give me feedback via email or on Mastodon.
